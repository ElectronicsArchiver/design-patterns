Confirmation: Already holding an offer?

Once you've received your exam results, follow these steps so that we can confirm your place at York.

Contact us

If you have any questions about the application process our Admissions team will be happy to help.

Undergraduate Admissions
ug-admissions@york.ac.uk

@uoystudy

Related links

  • Accommodation
You have an unconditional offer

If you've already accepted an unconditional offer at York - congratulations, you can relax! Your placed has been confirmed.

We're your firm choice and you meet all the conditions of our offer

If we already have your exam results and you meet the conditions of our offer we'll be able to make a final decision. Next you can check that UCAS Track has updated to confirm your place at York. UCAS will then send you a confirmation letter.

Well done, you can breathe a sigh of relief!

We're your firm choice but you have not met the conditions of our offer

If you don’t meet the conditions of our offer, please check UCAS Track. We may have been able to accept you or offer you a place on an alternative course.

We may not be able to let you know our decision straight away. In this case we’ll consider them alongside those of other applicants whose results are released on Thursday 13 August.

We're your insurance choice and you meet our conditions but not those of your firm choice

We're sorry that you didn’t get into your firm choice, however we’re delighted that you're coming to York and we're sure that you won’t regret it.

All you need to do is check that UCAS Track has updated to confirm your place, following this you’ll receive a confirmation letter from UCAS.

After you've confirmed your place

Say hello!

We're looking forward to meeting you in September. Follow @UniOfYork on Twitter and tell us how you're feeling about being a #YorkNewbie and say hello to your future fellow students!

Look out for your joining instructions

We'll keep in touch with you in the lead up to September to make your transition to university life as smooth as possible.

Look out for an e-mail entitled ‘Joining instructions’ (w/c 17 August 2015) which will give you instructions on how to upgrade your IT account so you can enrol.

Apply for accommodation

If you haven't already done so you should apply for accommodation. For more information visit:

  • Undergraduate accommodation

Prepare for your arrival

Take a look at our welcome information for new students to start planning your arrival in September!

  • New undergraduates

Still not got a place?

If you don’t meet the conditions of either your firm or insurance choice you may be able to find a place at York on an alternative course through Clearing.

To find out if you're in Clearing log into UCAS Track. If your status is ‘You are in Clearing’ you can then start to explore vacancies from Thursday 13 August 2015.

If you're applying for a re-mark

If you're applying for a re-mark, it’s really important that you let us know straight away at ug-admissions@york.ac.uk

Tell us which qualification and subject you're requesting your re-mark for and an estimated date of when you will receive the re-mark. We will not receive your re-mark automatically so make sure to let us know.

Remember to include your UCAS Personal ID in correspondence.
