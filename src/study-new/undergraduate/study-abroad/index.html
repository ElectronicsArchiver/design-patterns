Study and work abroad

At York, we aim to provide every student with an opportunity to gain an international experience.

Studying or working abroad during your degree is a life-changing experience. Not only will it enrich your educational experience, it will prepare you for a career in a competitive and global job market.

Why go abroad?

  • Standout from the crowd (less than 5% of UK students have studied abroad)
  • Boost your self-confidence, independence and ambition
  • Broaden your cultural and social perspectives
  • Conquer challenges, solve problems and discover new strengths and abilities
  • Develop your language skills
  • Spend a year abroad and save money with reduced tuition fees!

Destinations

York has links with top academic institutions and employers all over the world. Where you can go depends on your course of study at York.

However long you go for, you'll have a challenging and rewarding experience, see a difference culture and gain a new set of skills to impress potential employers.

Go for a full year or a semester

Study abroad as part of your course

  • Europe - Erasmus+: Apply for a place at one of our partner universities, or apply to work at an organisation related to your degree.
  • Courses with a built-in year abroad: We have a number of four-year courses with a built in year abroad.
  • Worldwide exchange programmes: You can study for a year at a partner university overseas.

Go for the summer or just a few weeks

Boost your employability skills

  • International Study Centres: Immerse yourself with a two to three week intensive academic and cultural experience at an international partner university.
  • Summer abroad: Do some volunteering or take a language and cultural exchange.
  • Travel awards: Apply for funding for independent travel experiences, such as a volunteering project or learning a language.
