Family accommodation

We have properties on and off campus to house families and we work with the York Housing Association (YHA) to provide even more family accommodation.

University properties

On campus (Garrowby Way)

In Halifax College we have have fully furnished houses which have a double bedroom, one single room and a small study space or third bedroom. We also have two bedroom flats, one of which is adapted for students with disabilities.

  • Price per night: £20.60
  • Price per week: £144.20
  • Internet included?: Yes
  • Bills included?: No
  • Advanced payment: £500 (deducted from your first rent instalment)

Payment is made in 12 monthly instalments. The first instalment will be for 12 nights only at a cost of £247.20 and the final instalment will be for 40 nights at a cost of £824.00. Throughout the rest of your let payments will be for 30/31 nights.

These properties are let for 51 weeks with no option to renew for a further year.

Off campus

We have a small number of fully furnished houses with three or four bedrooms that house students with larger families. Priority is given to families with three or more children. 

  • Price per night: £25.57
  • Price pe week: £178.99
  • Internet included?: Yes
  • Bills included?: No
  • Advanced payments: £500 (deducted from your first rent instalment)

Payment is made in 12 monthly instalments. The first instalment will be for 12 nights only at a cost of £306.84 and the final instalment will be for 40 nights at a cost of £1,022.80. Throughout the rest of your let payments will be for 30/31 nights.

York Housing Association properties

These furnished and unfurnished one-bedroom bungalows can be found in McHugh Court, which is close to Halifax College. As these aren't University properties they don't include bills and internet access, and your rent will be payable to YHA. You will have the option to renew your tenancy each year throughout the duration of your studies.

  • Prices: 2015/16 rent prices have not yet been confirmed
  • Internet included?: No
  • Bills included?: No
  • Advanced payments: 1 month*

*If you accept a YHA accommodation offer, you'll be asked to pay one month’s rent in advance. This will be returned to you when you leave the accommodation, dependant on the condition of the property.

How we allocate

We allocate on a first-come-first-served basis, based on the priority (highest to lowest) below:

  1. Students with evidence of a medical, welfare or mobility requirement
  2. New students from outside the EU
  3. New students from outside the UK but within the EU
  4. New students from the UK
  5. Continuing students who have not previously lived in University or YHA family accommodation
  6. Continuing students who have already lived in YHA family accommodation

Although our University accommodation is only available for a one-year let length, you may be able to extend your YHA contract beyond the initial let length, subject to availability. If your course is longer than one year we may give you priority for YHA accommodation.
