Private sector housing

We hope our accommodation will be your home away from home but if you would prefer to live off-campus there are plenty of student flats and houses in easy walking distance of the University.

Finding somewhere to live

We recommend the City of York Council's YorProperty scheme. YorProperty landlords have to attend yearly training, and the Council check 10% of properties each year. Look for 'YorPropertyPlus' homes, which are an even higher standard.

Not sure where to live? Take a look at these quick guides to York's residential areas.

  • YorProperty
  • Local quick guides

If you cannot find suitable accommodation on YorProperty, you can view a list of local agencies here:

  • Letting Agency list (PDF , 272kb)

Short term accommodation

Most student properties are available for a year, but if you're looking for a shorter let length we recommend our student advert service or the external Spare Room website:

  • Student adverts
  • Spare Room

Find a housemate

We hold a yearly event with the Graduate Students' Association to give you a chance to speak to other new students (usually postgraduates) and find someone to live with.

This year's event will take place on Friday 4 September 2015 (more details to follow).
