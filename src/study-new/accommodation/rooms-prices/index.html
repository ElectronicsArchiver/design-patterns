Families and couples
  • Families
  • Couples

All of our bedrooms include:

  • High speed internet access and wifi
  • Bed
  • Wardrobe
  • Desk and chair
  • Shelving and storage
  • Noticeboard
  • Ensuite bathroom with WC, sink and shower or a washbasin and access to a shared bathroom
  • Access to a shared kitchen

College facilities

All of our colleges have the following facilities in or close to the college:

  • 24/7 laundrette
  • Social spaces like common rooms, bars and dining halls
  • Computer rooms and/or study rooms

Find out more about college life

Campus facilities

As well as the facilities in your college, you'll also be close to the following campus facilities:

  • Library
  • A range of bars, cafés and restaurants
  • York Sports Centre (Heslington West), York Sport Village (Heslington East) and sports pitches
  • University computer rooms and study spaces
  • Grocery shops at Market Square and Halifax College
  • (YUSU) Students' Union shop selling drinks, snacks, magazines and merchandise
  • Barbecues
