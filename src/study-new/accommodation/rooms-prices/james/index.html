James

James College offers a mix of houses, flats and corridor-layout accommodation in a landscaped setting, with some lakeside views.

James has a large, lively dining hall and is home to the Student Unions’ cocktail bar, The Lounge.

College members shape and lead their community even before they arrive on campus, such as voting for their ‘Out of this World’ welcome event theme.

Quick facts

  • 590 rooms
  • £128 - 168 per week
  • Economy shared bathroom catered, Standard shared bathroom catered, Standard ensuite, Standard ensuite catered, Premium ensuite.
