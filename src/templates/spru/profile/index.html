Professor Bryony Beresford

Co-Director of SPRU
BSc (Hons) (Surrey); PhD (Exeter)

  • bryony.beresford@york.ac.uk
  • +44 (0)1904 321960
  • Social Policy Research Unit, University of York, Heslington, York YO10 5DD

Visit Professor Bryony Beresford's profile on the York Research Database to see publications, projects, collaborators, related work and more.

Research interests

  • Disabled or chronically ill children and adolescents and their families
  • Service provision and multi-agency working
  • Developing methodologies for research with children
  • Children's experiences of illness and disability
  • Issues and processes surrounding the implementation of research findings into practice

Current Research Projects

  • Disability, care and participation: secondary analysis of the Life Opportunities Survey and the 2009/10 Survey of Carers in Households
  • The Making Sense? Study: How do parents and young adults with Autistic Spectrum Disorders make sense of behaviour problems and mental health difficulties?
  • The SHAPE project: Supporting adults with High functioning Autism and asPerger syndromE: mapping and evaluating specialist autism team service models
  • Models of reablement evaluation: a mixed methods evaluation of a complex intervention (The MoRE project)
  • The PEAPIP project: Parents experiences of administering painful and invasive nursing procedures as part of caring for their ill child at home
