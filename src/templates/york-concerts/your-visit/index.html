Getting here

We are easily accessible at the heart of the University of York.

  • For daytime concerts, follow the footpath signs to Sir Jack Lyons Concert Hall
  • For evening concerts, follow the footpath signs to the Concerts & Evening Box Office
  • For accessible access: follow signs to Concerts & Evening Box Office and enter via the walkway to the Music Research Centre.

Parking

There are no parking charges for concert audiences after 6pm or at the weekend. At other times, please use pay and display car parks.

If you are visiting the Box Office in the afternoon, you may use one of the limited number of free short-stay spaces in the car park in front of the Information Centre.
