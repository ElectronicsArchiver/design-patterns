Bookings

Payment can be made by cash, cheque, Mastercard and Visa. If paying by cheque, please make your cheque payable to ‘University of York'.

Reservations are held for three working days or until 20 minutes before the start of the performance, whichever is sooner. We may not be able to take reservations within three days of a concert.

Booking fees

A booking fee of £1.50 applies to telephone and online transactions. No fee applies to tickets bought in person.

Booking online

Find your event in the What’s on section and select 'Book tickets' to make a purchase. You have the option to select your own seat from an interactive plan.

Booking by phone

Call the Box Office at +44 (0)1904 322439. If you get through to our voicemail, please leave a message and we'll return your call.

Booking in person, by post or email

  • In person from the Box Office
  • Post: send full details of your purchase, including a contact number and a cheque with a stamped, addressed envelope or add £1 to cover postage 
  • Email us at boxoffice@york.ac.uk and we'll call you to take payment.

Concessions

  • Companions Card - 50% discount on two tickets for essential carers attending concerts. To register, contact the Box Office.
  • Student - £5 or £3 tickets are available for students in full time education for most concerts. Please bring a valid NUS or student card when buying or collecting tickets or when bringing a print-at-home ticket.
  • Under 16s - free tickets when accompanied by an adult (one per adult full price or concession ticket bought). Additional tickets can be bought at the student price. These free tickets are not available online and should be booked through the Box Office. There are no free u16 tickets available in the Front Nave of York Minster; the £5 student ticket applies instead.
  • York Alumni - 10% discount for former students of the University of York. Booking conditions apply and this offer cannot be used in conjunction with other discounts.
  • Group bookings - Buy 9 tickets for any one concert and receive one additional ticket free. All tickets must be booked at the same time. School and college groups pay the student ticket price and receive a free ticket for a teacher/carer with every six student tickets bought.

Flexi-savings

We offer discounts for multiple bookings, so you can create your own series of concerts – the more you attend, the less you pay per concert.

  • Book three or four concerts - 10% discount on each ticket
  • Book five to seven concerts - 15% discount on each ticket
  • Book eight or more concerts - 20% discount on each ticket

    • You can make a multiple booking at any time up to, and including, the date of the first concert in your series. The offer is available for all prices, including concessions but excluding student tickets.

Refunds

We are unable to offer refunds on tickets already purchased. If an event sells out, we will try to re-sell your ticket(s) and if successful you'll receive a credit voucher which is valid until the date of the final concert of the season. There is an administation charge of £2 per transaction (£1 for FlexiSavings tickets).

All mobile devices must be switched off during performances and the unauthorised use of any cameras or other recording equipment is prohibited.

The consumption of alcohol is permitted inside the Sir Jack Lyons Concert Hall only at the discretion of the Duty Manager; the foyer is fully licenced. Consumption of alcohol in the Rymer and Central Hall is not permitted.
