Student finance

York's annual tuition fees cover the cost of your tuition, registration and exams. 

For some courses there are additional costs for field trips (check with your department). You will pay for your accommodation separately.

Your fee status

The level of fee that you will be asked to pay depends on whether you're classed as a UK, EU or international student.
