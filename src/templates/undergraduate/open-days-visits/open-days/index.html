Undergraduate Open Days

Explore what studying and living in York is really like at one of our Open Days.

Our staff and current students will be available to answer any questions you may have. 

Attend a mini-lecture and take a tour of our study facilities. Experience our campus for yourself and visit student accommodation.

Find out about life outside the lecture theatre, from sports clubs and societies to study abroad opportunities.

Other ways to meet us

  • Campus tours: Explore our campus on a guided or self-guided tour.
  • Visit days for offer-holders: We'll invite you to a visit day to help you decide whether York is for you. 
  • UCAS Conventions: Meet us at higher education fairs up and down the country.
  • Subject taster days: Take part in mini-lectures and meet staff and students.
  • Schools and colleges: We organise a variety of events for schools and colleges, on campus or at your school.
