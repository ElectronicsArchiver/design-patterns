Travel and parking

York is well connected through rail and road links, so we recommend that you try to use public transport where possible.

Travelling by train

Our campus is located about two miles from the train station.

Free shuttle bus

There's a free bus service for Open Day visitors between the train station and Campus Central at the University. Look out for specially-marked York Pullman buses on Station Road immediately outside the station.

The buses will run regularly from 8.45am. The journey will take about 20 minutes (allow up to 40 minutes if you're travelling at peak times).

Return buses to the train station will leave from Campus Central at Heslington West. The last bus will leave at 4.30pm.

From Heslington East, please board any bus in the direction of Campus Central at Heslington West. We recommend that you leave no later than 4pm to make sure you don't miss the last shuttle bus.

Buses from the train station

There are also regular service buses (numbers 4 and 44) which travel between the train station and our campus. Return tickets cost £2.00 per person.

Taxis from the train station

If you'd like to take a taxi from the train station or city centre ask the driver for Vanbrugh College at the University of York. The cost from the train station is approximately £8.00.

Travelling by car

Parking on campus is very limited during Open Days; we cannot guarantee that you'll be able to find a space and you'll have to pay and display. Instead please make use of the free Open Day park and ride service.

Parking for visitors with disabilities

If you have a valid disabled parking permit you can park for free in designated disabled spaces in any of the University car parks. Security staff will be available for assistance.

Free park and ride service

A free Open Day park and ride service is provided at the Yorkshire Air Museum (approximately fives miles from campus).

How to get to the Yorkshire Air Museum

The postcode for satnav purposes is YO41 4AU.

  • Take the outer ring road (A64 on the south and east sides of the city, A1237 round the north and west) to the junction with Elvington Lane (B1228).
  • From this junction the route to the Yorkshire Air Museum will be signposted.

Free shuttle bus

The buses will run regularly from the Yorkshire Air Museum to the University from 8.45am. There are two drop off points:

  • The first stop is Ron Cooke Hub at Heslington East
  • The second stop is Campus Central at Heslington West

Return buses to the Yorkshire Air Museum leave from Campus Central at Heslington West. The last bus will leave from Campus Central at 4.30pm.
