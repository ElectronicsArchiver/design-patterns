Confirmation, Adjustment and Clearing 2015

Looking for a late place?

Some of our courses are still open for applications and we expect to have places available for well-qualified students when A level results come out. Take a look at our courses in the following areas:

  • Accounting
  • Archaeology
  • Economics and Related Studies
  • Education
  • Environment
  • Electronics
  • History of Art
  • Language and Linguistic Science
  • Management
  • Mathematics
  • Music
  • Nursing
  • Philosophy
  • Physics
  • Politics
  • Politics, Economics and Philosophy
  • Social and Political Sciences
  • Social Policy
  • Sociology
  • Theatre, Film and Television
    • Interactive Media
    • Writing, Directing and Performance

Not holding any offers?

If you're not currently holding any offers you can call our Admissions team now on +44 (0) 1904 323536 to find out which courses are open for applications.

Waiting for your results?

If you're waiting for your results you can sign up now for an alert and we'll email you on A level results day (Thursday 13 August) with details of our opportunities in Adjustment and Clearing.
