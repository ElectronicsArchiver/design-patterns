Undergraduate accommodation

With all of our accommodation on or very near to campus, you'll be living right at the heart of the action.

Our guarantee

Whether we're your firm or insurance choice, you’re guaranteed a room booking if you apply for accommodation by 31 July 2015 and become unconditional by Friday 21 August (12 noon BST).

Our guarantee applies to all new, full-time, first year undergraduate students applying for a single room for the full academic year.

If you pay overseas tuition fees, we can guarantee you a room booking for each year of your course if you apply by our published deadline dates for the full letting period.

Quick facts

  • £103 to £168 per week (single rooms)
  • Heating, electricity, water, contents insurance and wifi included
  • Over half of our rooms have been built or refurbished since 2009
  • You'll find bars, cafés, dining halls, launderettes, secure bike sheds, common rooms and study areas on your doorstep

Colleges

You will be a member of one of our eight undergraduate colleges throughout your time here. Our colleges are much more than just halls of residence. As well as social, study and catering facilities, they also offer:

  • Support and guidance through a dedicated team of tutors and staff
  • Activities and events that bring together students, staff and alumni
  • Opportunities to meet new people and try new experiences

Although some departments are located within college buildings, you don’t need to apply for a particular college based on your course. Our campus location means that all our colleges are a short walk to our departments and teaching buildings.

  • Colleges, rooms and prices

Families and couples

Most of our rooms are for individual applicants, but if you wish to live with your partner and/or children, you can apply for our studio flats or houses. These aren't guaranteed, but we will try our best to help you.

  • Families
  • Couples

Nursing and Midwifery students

As your course will involve work placements and shift working, you’ll be housed in a self-catered flat with other Nursing and Midwifery students in one of five colleges (currently Alcuin, Constantine, Derwent, Halifax or James).

  • Rooms and prices

Hull York Medical School (HYMS) students

Although you won't yet have been allocated to a university, you should apply for University of York accommodation now. We'll contact you with a room offer if you're allocated to York.

  • Accommodation at HYMS

Beyond your first year

If you love living in your college, you can apply to live on campus for each year that you're here. You can apply individually or with your friends, and benefit from hassle-free payment with no bills or hidden costs to worry about.
