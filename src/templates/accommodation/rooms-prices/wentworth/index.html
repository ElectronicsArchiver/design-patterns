Wentworth College

Our dedicated postgraduate college offers ensuite rooms, many with great views across the lake. Wentworth also has some economy rooms just a short walk from campus.

You'll have plenty of spaces for studying and socialising, including a computer room, a graduate study space, an art studio, and The Edge bar and restaurant.

Each Christmas, tutors and students come together to eat mince pies and decorate the college Christmas tree with messages to friends and family.

Quick facts

  • 500 rooms
  • £103 - £161 per week
  • Premium ensuite, Studio
