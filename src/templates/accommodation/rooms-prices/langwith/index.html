Langwith College

Langwith moved to new buildings in 2012. Its Premium accommodation has a mix of flats and houses.

At the heart of Langwith is The Glasshouse bar, run by the Students’ Union. You also have access to common rooms in neighbouring Constantine and Goodricke Colleges.

Get behind your college and wear your black and gold at Langwith’s sporting battles against its Durham University sister college, Josephine Butler!

Quick facts

  • 630 rooms
  • £126 - £161
  • Premium shared bathroom, Premium ensuite, Studio

Prices

All undergraduate rooms are let for 40 weeks, unless stated otherwise.
