Couples accommodation

We have a small number of on-campus rooms and studios for couples, and also work with the York Housing Association (YHA) to provide some one bedroom properties.

University properties: On campus

Derwent and Vanbrugh College both offer ensuite double rooms with their own bathroom and access to a shared kitchen.

We also have self-contained flats and studio flats in Derwent, Goodricke, Langwith (shown above), Vanbrugh and Wentworth Colleges, which have their own kitchen facilities as well as a private bathroom.

Rooms and prices

Bills and wifi are included with all room options.

  • Ensuite double room: £135.24 per week
  • Couples flat: £144.20 per week
  • Studio flat: £161.14 per week

There is a £300 advance payment for University accommodation, which will be deducted from your first rent instalment.

  • Rooms and prices

York Housing Association properties

These furnished and unfurnished one-bedroom bungalows can be found in McHugh Court, which is close to Halifax College. As these aren't University properties they don't include bills and wifi, and your rent will be payable to YHA. You will have the option to renew your tenancy each year throughout the duration of your studies.

Rooms and prices

Bills and wifi are not included.

  • One bedroom unfurnished bungalow: £114.18 per week*
  • One bedroom furnished bungalow: £123.53 per week**

*11 monthly payments of approximately £529.09 per month, paid in three termly instalments

**11 monthly payments of approximately £572.73 per month, paid in three termly instalments

If you accept a YHA accommodation offer, you'll be asked to pay one month’s rent in advance. This will be returned to you when you leave the accommodation, dependant on the condition of the property.

How we allocate

We allocate on a first-come-first-served basis, based on the priority (highest to lowest) below:

  1. Students with evidence of a medical, welfare or mobility requirement
  2. New students from outside the EU
  3. New students from outside the UK but within the EU
  4. New students from the UK
  5. Continuing students who have not previously lived in University or YHA family accommodation
  6. Continuing students who have already lived in YHA family accommodation

Although our University accommodation is only available for a one-year let length, you may be able to extend your YHA contract beyond the initial let length, subject to availability. If your course is longer than one year we may give you priority for YHA accommodation.

Private sector accommodation

As we can't guarantee accommodation for families and couples, you should also look into private sector accommodation around York.
