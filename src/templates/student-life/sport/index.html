Sport and fitness

Whether you want to participate, play, prepare or perform there's something for everyone at York - from beginners to pros. You can indulge existing passions or try something completely new.

What's on offer:

  • Be part of York Sport Union, the sporting arm of the Students' Union (YUSU) - they coordinate 60+ sports clubs, 3000+ players and 16 college sports.
  • Keep fit and train in our flagship facilities which include our £9m Sport Village.
  • Take part in sports events and competitions including Europe's largest inter-university competition, Roses and our annual College Varsity competition.
  • Stay active on our green campus with plenty of cycling and walking tracks, or take part in York Active, which provides sport and fitness classes for all ability levels.
  • Walk, cycle or run our new YorActive Trail, a 5km outdoor fitness trail connecting the west and east parts of campus.
  • Inclusive opportunities for all students to participate in sport regardless of race, sexual orientation, nationality or disability status.

College sports

If casual sport is your thing, college sport is for you.

It's open to everyone whether you've been playing a sport for years or you fancy trying something new. Play for fun or compete against other colleges.

You could also play for your college against Durham University, in the only inter-university college varsity.

Find College Sport at York on Facebook

York Active

York Active is a fun way to play sport in a non-competitive and relaxed environment, no matter what your ability level.

Choose from sports such as netball, badminton, hula hooping and rave spin! You can also play ping pong on free outdoor table tennis on campus.

York Active is a three-year Sport England funded programme which aims to increase sport and physical activity among York students and staff.

Annual sporting events

There are plenty of sporting events on offer whether you want to take part in the action or sit back and spectate.

Other key events:

  • College Varsity, between York and Durham University, is the only inter-university college sport tournament in the UK.
  • Our annual College Sports Day is our most nostalgic and fun event of the year involving a range of old school sporting events.
  • York Sport Union Colours Ball, the pinnacle event of the sporting year, celebrates the fantastic achievements of our University sports clubs over the year.
  • The College Sport Celebrations recognise and reward the fantastic students who participate in college sport.
  • Freshers' Fair, where all the University sports clubs will be out in force and looking to entice you into join their club!
"Roses for me as a student was the pinnacle weekend of my university experience. It’s basically like a mini Olympics. It’s the one weekend of the year when all clubs properly unite together for one goal, to beat Lancaster!"

First-class facilities on campus

York Sport Village and Sports Centre give you access to a wide range of first-class sport, health and fitness facilities, services and programmes. You can choose from a range of pay-as-you-go and membership options.

Explore the full range of facilities on offer (york-sport.com).

YorActive outdoor fitness trail

Run, walk or cycle the new YorActive Trail and use outdoor gym equipment along the way.

The new 5km outdoor fitness trail, open to students, staff and the wider community, will connect the west and east parts of campus.

It should be up and running in the summer.
