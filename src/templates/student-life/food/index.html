Food and drink on campus

Campus is packed with places to eat and drink including several Students' Union (YUSU) venues. For your groceries and essentials we also have two supermarkets and one YUSU shop.

Student favourites

  1. The Courtyard in Derwent College is one the most popular places to eat and drink on campus serving up homemade burgers and cheesy chips.
  2. The Glasshouse in Langwith College on Campus East serves up nachos, panini and wraps. They also have curry club once a week and a late night menu with cocktails.
  3. The Kitchen at Alcuin has a vintage feel with big comfy sofas and is recommended as the place to go for a cuppa and a slice of cake.
  4. The Lounge in James College is a favourite for cocktails and society socials.
  5. Vanbrugh CollegeVanbrugh Food Court offers burritos with veggie and meat options as well as healthy Asian broths.
  6. At the Galleria Restaurant at Roger Kirk you can get noodles, halal food and a hearty breakfast.
  7. Derwent College has a popular world street food menu from curry to falafels.
  8. The Scoop Student Co-operatative is a student shop offering healthy, ethical, local goods on a budget.
  9. There is also a food market once a month on Market Square serving up tasty homemade treats.
  10. For a pub lunch nip to The Charles or The Deramore Arms in Heslington Village.

Top tip: all of the profit from the Students' Union (YUSU) venues goes back into funding student activities including societies and sports clubs.

Catered accommodation and meal deals

Catered accommodation is available for undergraduate students which includes breakfast and a two course meal. We also have a Meals in Advance Deal available to all students, where you can buy discounted meals on a term by term basis.
