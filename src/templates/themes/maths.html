Life in York

University life is about much more than the lecture theatre. You'll make friendships that could last a lifetime. Through societies, sports clubs and college life, you'll get to harness your passions, try something new and you'll gain a wealth of experience that will impress future employers.

Accommodation

We offer a range of campus accommodation to suit you and your budget, from economy to deluxe.

Student life

Explore campus and city life and hear what our current students have to say about living here.

The city

Our beautiful and historic city is regularly voted one of the best places to live in the UK.

Life on campus

Our compact green campus provides a safe and friendly setting and is within easy reach of the city centre.

Colleges

You’ll join one of our eight colleges, where you'll be part of a community from day one.

Sport

Keep fit and train in our £9m sport village and choose from over 60 sports clubs from netball to football and octopush and ultimate frisbee.

International students

23% of our students are from outside the UK, representing over 160 nationalities.

Preparing for your future

From the moment you arrive at York, we provide you with opportunities that will enhance your learning and set you up for a bright future in whatever you want to do.

Teaching and learning

Our research-led teaching means you'll be taught by academics who are involved in research that changes the world.

Your future career

We'll ensure you're equipped to compete with the brightest graduates for the best jobs, all over the world.

Study abroad
