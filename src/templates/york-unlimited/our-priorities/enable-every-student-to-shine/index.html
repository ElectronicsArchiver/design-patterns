Scholarships

We know that talented students can achieve their potential whatever their background and family circumstances.  We also know that students from low income households, who grow up in areas of deprivation, or are first in their family to come to university, need additional support.  

Scholarships level the playing field. They enable the students who stand to gain the most from higher education to take advantage of all it has to offer. But more than this: scholarships help to instil confidence in our students that comes from knowing that someone believes in them.

Our flagship scholarships are funded by York’s alumni and friends community through philanthropic donations:

York Opportunity Scholarship

Support students who have faced significant barriers in their personal or social life.

Read more

York Futures Scholarship

Support students to access opportunities that give them a head start in the job market.

Read more

How can I help?

  • Help students overcome barriers to study at York through supporting the York Opportunity Scholarship.
  • Encourage students to pursue new experiences and opportunities through the York Futures Scholarships.
  • Support one of our other scholarships.
